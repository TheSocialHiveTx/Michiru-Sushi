About Michiru Sushi

Serving the Clear Lake area since 2009, MichiRu Sushi offers unparalleled quality, service, and creativity at an exceptional value. While maintaining the focus on detail and presentation, our chefs employ new and traditional techniques with unique seasonal ingredients from around the world, to continue the evolution of sushi fusion and modern Asian cuisine. Boasting the city’s best reward program, a warm comfortable ambiance, a special menu that changes weekly, MichiRu provides a fulfilling dining experience.
